About Team S.A.F.E.

Team S.A.F.E. (Smart Analytics for Fire Emergencies) We are graduate students from San Diego State University’s Big Data Analytics program, dedicated to helping communities respond to wildfires through data-driven insights. Our platform provides real-time shelter locations, fire hazard mapping, and critical resources to empower individuals and agencies during emergencies. Our mission is to keep LA County residents informed, safe, and supported through technology, open data, and accessible design.
