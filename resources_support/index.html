  • California Community Foundation: Supports rebuilding, mental health, and underserved recovery. Donate or apply

Protect Yourself from Wildfire Smoke and Ash

Guidance on protecting yourself from wildfire smoke and ash exposure is provided by the South Coast AQMD. These tips are essential for individuals with asthma, heart disease, or other respiratory issues, as well as for families with children and the elderly.
